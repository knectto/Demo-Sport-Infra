FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your sports infrastructure project. Still have questions? Call us — consultation is always free.

How long does a typical court project take?+
Standard single-court projects (tennis, basketball, badminton) take 3–4 weeks. Football turf fields take 4–6 weeks. Running tracks and multi-court complexes take 6–10 weeks depending on site conditions, soil type, and weather.
What certifications do your surfaces have?+
Artificial turf — FIFA approved. Badminton flooring — BWF approved. Tennis surfaces — ITF certified. Running tracks — IAAF certified. Basketball courts — built to FIBA specifications. All materials come from certified international manufacturers.
Do you provide a warranty on completed projects?+
Yes. All projects come with a full warranty covering both the surface material and installation workmanship. We also offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) for ongoing brushing, infill top-up, drainage care, and minor repairs.
How much does an artificial turf football field cost?+
A standard 5-a-side box turf starts from approximately ₹8–12 lakhs. A full 7-a-side field ranges from ₹18–35 lakhs. Pricing depends on field size, turf quality, sub-base, fencing, and lighting. Call us for a free, site-specific quote.
Do you serve smaller cities outside metros?+
Absolutely. We have completed projects in all 28+ states including tier-2 and tier-3 cities. Our teams operate nationwide and maintain the same quality standards everywhere. Travel costs may apply for very remote locations.
What is the lifespan of artificial turf?+
With proper maintenance, FIFA-approved artificial turf lasts 8–12 years. Lifespan depends on usage intensity, quality of yarn and backing, infill maintenance, and UV exposure. We recommend an AMC plan to maximise surface life.
Can you build multi-sport on a single surface?+
Yes. We offer multi-sport court configurations where a single surface is line-marked for multiple games — e.g. basketball + volleyball + badminton on one court, or multi-sport turf for outdoor spaces. We'll advise on the best option for your needs.
Is the site survey and design really free?+
Yes — 100% free, no obligation. Our team visits, measures, and provides full CAD design + itemised quote at no charge. We believe in complete transparency from day one before any commitment is made.
